## Usage and Details

- Despite Zero swinging his sword downward, it has a decent hitbox above it allowing it to be a great rising air normal.
- Also works well as a falling button.
- One of Zero's go to air-to-airs. This button is fantastic at a catching opponents moving around in the air.
  Unlike <InlineInput color="red" text="j.H" /> and <InlineInput color="red" text="j.2H" />, <InlineInput color="yellow" text="j.M" /> is special cancelable.
  This means you get easy superjump confirms with <InputBlock><Text color="yellow">j.M</Text> xx Level 3 Buster Release, <Text color="blue">j.214L</Text></InputBlock>.
