## Usage and Details

- Zero's non-invincible DP.
- Used as combo filler to optimize some routes by doing <InputBlock><Text color="red">236H</Text> xx Air Buster Release, <Text color="blue">j.623L</Text></InputBlock>.
- Ryuenjin H has the most largest and most active hitbox of the three.
- It's utility in neutral is much better than the L and M versions. It functions decently as an anti-air. If combined with Jam Session, you can get easy confirms with Level 3 Buster.
