## Usage and Details

- Unlike the grounded version, Air Hienkyaku L sends Zero down to the floor.
- It's a very good tool at keeping Zero mobile, allowing you to go from poking in the air and back to the ground in an instant.
- Used a lot in confirming from super jump height after a Level 3 Buster hit.
- It can be used to create empty jump low mixups while the opponent is blocking an assist. <InputBlock>Normal Jump, j.214L, 2L</InputBlock> is a very potent empty jump low setup that hits even the most experienced players.
