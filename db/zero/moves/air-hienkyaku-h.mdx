## Usage and Details

- Can pass through opponents.
- Functions the same as <Link href="/zero/moves/hienkyaku-m">Hienkyaku H</Link>.
- Even though there is a animation for his downward trajectory, you can cancel the dash with any air action at the apex of the command dash (i.e air dash or an air normal).
  This is especially useful because it can get you up to superjump height without actually superjumping giving you access to assists.
