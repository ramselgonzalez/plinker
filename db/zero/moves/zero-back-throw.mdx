## Usage and Details

- Throws and throw techs resets Zero's buster charge.
- If _you_ have your back to the corner when you throw the opponent, you can confirm from back throw with <InputBlock>j.236H</InputBlock> and an assist or X-Factor.
- Midscreen, you can confirm with <InputBlock>214M xx X-Factor Cancel, j.236M, 2L</InputBlock>.
  It's a tough link but doable with practice.
