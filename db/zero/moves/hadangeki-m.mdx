## Usage and Details

- Zero's standard fireball.
- Projectile - 3 low durability points.
- Hadangeki M starts up and travels the fastest of the three versions. This will be your go to fireball in most situations.
- It has slower recovery than Hadangeki L but the same recovery of Hadangeki H.
- In Sougenmu, you can create a near perfect blockstring from fullscreen by repeatedly doing Hadangeki M. This is great option to either apply a lot of chip damage or time up an
  opponent's X-Factor or other install supers.
