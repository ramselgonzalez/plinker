## Usage and Details

- One of Zero's divekicks.
- Zero's primary OTG move.
- Travels down at a 45 degree angle.
- Causes a hard knockdown if used in a launcher combo.
- A good tool to quickly reach the ground with a hitbox out. Alternating this with <InputBlock>j.236M</InputBlock> can make your downward trajectory hard to read
  and can score you some mixups if behind an assist.
