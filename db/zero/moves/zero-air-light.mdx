## Usage and Details

- A quick air normal.
- Has most utility as a quick falling normal to disrespect an opponent's pressure after pushblocking.
- Not super effective as an air-to-air.
- Can be used to instant overhead most of the cast while standing and big bodies while crouching. You can get a full combo from the instand
  overhead with <InputBlock><Text color="blue">j.L</Text> xx Level 3 Buster Release, <Text color="blue">j.214L</Text></InputBlock>.
