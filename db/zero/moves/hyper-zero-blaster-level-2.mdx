## Usage and Details

- Commonly referred to as "Buster".
- Projectile - 4 low durability points.
- Requires 70 frames of charging of any attack button.
- You can transfer your charge to another button as long as the initial button is not released.
- Travels faster, does more damage, and has better frame data than Level 1 Buster.
- Has better uses than in neutral. When mixed in with <InlineInput color="yellow" text="236M" />, in can frame trap
  opponents who try to advance in or input specials after blocking Hadangeki.
