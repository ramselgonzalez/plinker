## Purpose

This combo is to understand how Zero can relaunch midscreen without an assist. <InputBlock>OTG <Text color="red">j.236H</Text> xx Air Buster Release, <Text color="blue">j.214L</Text>, Dash Forward <Text color="green">j.S</Text></InputBlock>
is a universal relaunch. Depending on the starter, you can add <InlineInput color="yellow" text="5M"/> or <InlineInput color="red" text="5H" /> after the <InlineInput color="blue" text="j.214L"/> for a little bit of extra damage/meter and
some leniency in the timing instead of going straight for the <InlineInput color="green" text="5S"/>.
